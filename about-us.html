Who We Are

We are leading export oriented company who supplies all high-class fresh produce, vegetables, fruits & Spices based in Up Country, Central & Low Country Sri Lanka.

Our ethos is to provide exceptional standards of quality and service in all areas of our business and the core business is the supply of high-class fresh produce.

Our company is also dedicated to fulfilling our duties to the Sri Lankan economy as well as the community. We are pleased in supporting farmers in their farming activities as many of our suppliers are being small scale family farmers whom we consider our family.

Our Mission

To inspire people to choose a healthier, greener, more compassionate lifestyle through plant-based eating. To produce and sell products that enhance quality of life and well-being.

Our Vision

To be a premier innovator of natural food flavor and ingredient solutions to the world’s leading food marketers and manufacturers.
